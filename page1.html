Burning issue

Every summer water table goes down in many regions of the country due to increased population as well as decreasing rainfall,As the water table goes down and there is a shortage of drinking water people who are living on rent vacate such colonies and this hits the economy as well.

For getting pure drinking water each household spends additionally on water purifiers water is a common necessity for every individual still there are areas in our country where people have to travel long distances just to get a glass of pure drinking water.

A child who is supposed to be busy with studies in villages is now busy bringing drinking water from far-off places as water that is coming from community tap or tubewell is not fit for drinking.
